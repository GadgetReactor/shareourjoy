Our Services

Live Interaction at Your Events

At Share Our Joy, we believe in offering a unique edge to your event. Let your guests participate in the action! We specialize in mobile interactive services and offer interactive entertainment at your events. SMS to Screen and Live Pictures Slideshow are but two ways to let the crowd send texts and pictures to the big screen from their mobile phones via SMS, Email, Facebook or Twitter and participate in the fun.

 

What Do We Offer?

Live Photography
Our live photographers are equipped with WIFI enabled cameras and can wirelessly and instantly send photos to be projected on the screen within seconds and printed out on credit card sized photocards within minutes for the guests to keep. Alternatively, they can also download the high-quality photos from our website. Live Photography adds so much more interaction to the event, making it more fun, entertaining and most of all, memorable.

 

Interactive Text Banner
We can display a live chat on the big screen where people can participate by sending text messages from their mobile phones via SMS or messages from a Facebook Wall. We call it Instant SMS or Live SMS, allowing the guests at the events to send words of wishes to the happy couple for example. The Live SMS banner an be combined with the other features to make it a unique and special event!

 

Live picture publishing
People at your event can send in pictures via Facebook or Email and viola! They will be published on the big screen.

 

Message moderation
If you are worried out unwanted messages appearing on screen, this can be control through manual authorization to review each received message before publishing it on the projection screen. Our staff will be on site to ensure that the messages and photos are filtered.

 

Multimedia integration
The text banner, photos, can be integrated with your desired multimedia content. videos, photos, and more.

 

Customizable
Design your own? Have an idea of what you want to do but can’t figure out? Speak to our consultants and let them do the dirty work in making your event a success! Contact us now.
