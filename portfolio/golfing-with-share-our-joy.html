We were engaged for our first corporate event and it was off to the golf course. It was a bright sunny day and it was challenging to ensure that the photos were not overexposed. Glad that we suceeded. Taking live sports event is always interesting due to the unexpected nature of it. Our clients were impressed that we were able to capture the intense determination showcased by some of the golfers as well as the moments of joy or disappointment after they hit their shots. We were glad they liked the photos!
